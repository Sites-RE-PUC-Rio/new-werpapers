Experimenting a Requirements Engineering Process Based on Rational Unified Process (RUP) Reaching Capability Maturity Model Integration (CMMI) Maturity Level 3 and Considering the Use of Agile Methods Practices

Caroline Carbonell Cintra; Roberto Tom Price

Workshop on Requirements Engineering (WER)2006

Leer PDFVer ediciónGoogle Scholar

Resumen

This work has the purpose of describing a software development process with the following characteristics: its scope lies within the requirements engineering activities; it fulfills CMMI requisites for Requirements Management and Requirements Development process areas (maturity level 2 and 3, respectively); it is based on RUP practices and activities where possible practices of agile methods are employed. Related work is considered and similarities as well as differences to the process proposed here are pointed out. Such process is presented in terms of each of its activity flows, including mention to the artifacts and roles involved on the activities. CMMI fulfillment is also described, followed by the description of the main contributions achieved by this work and comments on future development.
