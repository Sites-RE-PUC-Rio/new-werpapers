A Study in Market-Driven Requirements Engineering

Carina Alves; Silvia Pereira; Jaelson Castro

Workshop on Requirements Engineering (WER)2006

Leer PDFVer ediciónGoogle Scholar

Resumen

Packaged software is recognised as an important segment of software market. Market-driven software development consists of developing packaged software to a market rather than to a specific client. It has been argued that the requirements engineering process for market-driven software differs from customer specific software. This paper presents a study on market-driven requirements engineering. We present ten hypotheses we have collected from the literature in this field. We discuss the implications of these hypotheses and identify areas to conduct further research.
