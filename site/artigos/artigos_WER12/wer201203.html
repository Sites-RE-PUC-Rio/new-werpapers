Bula de Software: Uma Estrutura Definida para Promover a Melhoria da Transparência em Software

André Luiz de Castro Leal; Eduardo Almentero; Herbet Cunha; Henrique P. Sousa; Julio Cesar S. do P. Leite

Workshop on Requirements Engineering (WER)2012

Leer PDFVer ediciónGoogle Scholar

Resumen

This article presents a novel proposal for software documentation that is founded on package insert (patient information leaflets) for drugs. The objective of our proposal, called Software Bula, is to provide for users and developers software information that are at the same time clean and well structured and organized.

Palabras clave

Transparency; documentation; software.
