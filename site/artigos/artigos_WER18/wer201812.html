A Catalogue of iStar Extensions

Enyo Gonçalves; Tiago Heineck; João Araújo; Jaelson Castro

Workshop on Requirements Engineering (WER)2018DOI 10.17771/PUCRio.wer.inf2018-5

Leer PDFVer ediciónGoogle Scholar

Resumen

iStar has been extended since its initial proposal in the 90s. It is worth noting that since 2016 the language notation is under standardisation. However, new extensions continue to be proposed. The search for previous ex-tensions and its constructs can be a starting point in the proposal of new ones. So, to ease the identification of the previous extensions and its constructs is es-sential to next proposals. Motivated by this situation, the objective of this paper is to present a catalogue of iStar extensions. The extensions and their constructs were identified from a systematic literature review, which identified that 96 ex-tensions had been proposed until 2016. The results suggest that catalogue is im-portant during future iStar extensions proposal.
