Aplicação de Técnicas de Leitura durante a Análise de Requisitos

Márcia Seabra Cabral; Fernanda Alencar; Jaelson Castro; Oscar Pastor; Juan Sánchez

Workshop on Requirements Engineering (WER)2008

Leer PDFVer ediciónGoogle Scholar

Resumen

This paper describes the result work of the reading techniques application, Checklist and PBR, in the requirements analysis activity during the inspection process to help the inspectors to detect defects. The objective is to analyze the reading technique effectiveness. It presents the experiment result performed in the AMADeUs-MM requirements. será aceito ou não após a atividade de Validação de Requisitos.
