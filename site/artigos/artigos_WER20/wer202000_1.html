Resumen

The Workshop on Requirements Engineering 2020 is now in its twentieth-third edition. This series of workshops on the area started in 1998 as a meeting of the Ibero-American requirements engineering community, but along the years it has attracted attention from researchers and practitioners from other parts of the world
