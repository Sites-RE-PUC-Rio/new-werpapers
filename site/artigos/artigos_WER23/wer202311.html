Visual-PR: Uma abordagem visual e gamificada para o apoio à Priorização de Requisitos

Luiza Lira; Maria Lencastre; Joao Henrique Pimentel; Jaelson Brelaz Castro; Mônica Bandeira

Workshop on Requirements Engineering (WER)2023DOI 10.29327/1298356.26-5

Leer PDFVer ediciónGoogle Scholar

Resumen

This article aims to mitigate some barriers to adopting requirements prioritization, such as the complexity of the activity and the need for more engagement and interaction among its participants. The proposed process, called Visual-PR, integrates visual and gamified models and tools to support PR, ranging from early to late requirements. A case study demonstrated the process's use-fulness in supporting and motivating prioritization

Palabras clave

Priorização; Requisitos; Gamificação; Visualização
